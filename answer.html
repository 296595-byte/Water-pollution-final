Water Pollution

Top 3 asked questions we get

What are the causes?

Water pollutions stems from many things that you may think aren't that bad.

  • Things like plastics, oil spills, other harmful substances,agricultural runoff, and not recycling properly.
Who does it affect?

Water pollutions affects more than you think. even when a little is put in the ocean it can affect many different species.

  • Some marine animals that are affected are seals, turtles, fish, sharks, starfish, and dophins. it can hurt them by choking, injuring, separating them from their family, blocking their systems causing them to starve.
  • Not only does it affect marine life, it affects humans to by reducing finding fresh water, threatening common marine animals that are used for selling and making money, and can spread diseases (especially in young children)
What are some solutions?

They're are many solutions to reduce the amount of pollution in bodies of water.

  • Things like recycling, picking up any garbage near a water area, and choosing ECO-friendly products can make such a difference.
