Water Pollution

Top 3 asked questions we get about water pollution.

What are the causes?

Water pollutions stems from many things that you may think aren't that bad.

  • Things like plastics, oil spills, other harmful substances,agricultural runoff, and not recycling properly.
Who does it affect?

Water pollutions affects more than you think. even when a little is put in the ocean it can affect many different species.

  • it can hurt them by choking,injuring, and cause them to starve.
What are some solutions?

They're are many solutions to reduce the amount of pollution in bodies of water.

  • Things like recycling, picking up any garbage, and choosing ECO-friendly products.
