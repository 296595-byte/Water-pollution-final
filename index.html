Turning the tides on pollution.

We all know water pollution is "bad." But can you articulate precisely how adding a specific type of pollution impacts a single marine organism's life cycle?

Our Reason

Water pollution is a very common environmental challenge we have been dealing with for many years now, resulting in the danger of many marine animal species like seals,sea turtles,fish,sharks, and dolphins. Lots of unwanted things are put into the ocean, like pervasive plastic pollution (bags, bottles, fishing gear.) The estimated amount of garbage in oceans are about 75-199 million tons, in their homes!

So, our goal is to try and reduce this amount, you can even to it too! Some ways we can lower the amount of water pollution is by simply recycling, picking up any garbage near water areas, and choosing ECO-friendly products instead of plastic ones.
